QR Code Information

QR codes are not included in the deployment to save space and stay within free tier limits.

How to get QR codes:

QR codes can be generated locally and printed for use on your motors. Follow these steps:

  1. Clone the repository to your local machine
  2. Make sure the BASE_URL is set to: https://bof-jindal-equipment-management-system.onrender.com/motor?id=
  3. Run python simple_excel_import.py to generate QR codes
  4. Print the QR codes from the qr_codes folder

This approach keeps the deployment light while allowing you to use QR codes locally.
