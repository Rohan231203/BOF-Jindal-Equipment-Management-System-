QR Code Information

QR codes can be generated in two ways: on the deployed app or locally on your development machine.

Option 1: Adding Motors on the Deployed App

You can add motors directly through the web interface:

  1. Go to the Home page
  2. Enter a new Motor ID in the form and click "Generate QR & Add Motor"
  3. Access the QR code image at: https://bof-jindal-equipment-management-system.onrender.com/qr_codes/MOTORID.png
  4. Download and print the QR codes for physical attachment to motors

Option 2: Bulk Generation Locally

For generating multiple QR codes at once from Excel data:

  1. Clone the repository to your local machine
  2. Ensure the BASE_URL is set to: https://bof-jindal-equipment-management-system.onrender.com/motor?id=
  3. Run python simple_excel_import.py to generate QR codes from your Excel file
  4. Print the QR codes from the qr_codes folder

Both methods will create valid QR codes that point to your deployed application.
